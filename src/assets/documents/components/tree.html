Best Practices Best Practices
Trees are a continuous group of text or control components. Trees should be:
  • logical: Trees should be sorted in logical ways that make content easy to scan, such as alphabetical, numerical, chronological, or by user preference.
  • Actionable: Trees present content in a way that makes it easy to identify a specific item in a collection and act on it.
Usage & Content Guidelines Content Guidelines
Don’t place imagery in tree that makes the content hard to scan.
