Best Practices Best Practices
Expansion panels provides extra information in details-summary view of its header, that’s easy to scan, so that users can look for patterns and insights.
Expansion panels should:
  • Focused: Expansion panels focus user attention to ensure their content is addressed.
  • Direct: Expansion panels should be direct in communicating information and dedicated to completing a task, allow lightweight editing of an element.
