Slide Toggle
Slider Toggles are used for binary actions. They are commonly used for “On/Off” situations.
Examples Examples
Basic slide-toggles
Default slide toggle is an on/off control that can be toggled via clicking or dragging.

Best Practices Best Practices
Slider toggle should:
  • Control over an option or feature that can be turned on or off.
  • Be made clear from the corresponding inline label for the option that it controls.
Usage & Content Guidelines Content Guidelines
