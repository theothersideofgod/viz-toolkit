Sidenav
The sidenav components are designed to provide access to destinations in your app. They can either be permanently on-screen or controlled by a navigation menu icon.
Examples Examples
Sidenavs are anchored to the left or right edge of the screen. They are a fixed width and typically span the height of the screen. Their dimensions depend on how the app’s layout is subdivided into UI regions.

Best Practices Best Practices
Sidenav should:
  • The placement and list-style content of navigation drawers clearly identify them as navigation.
  • Sidenavs order destinations according to user importance, with frequent destinations first and related ones grouped together.
  • Contextual: Sidenavs can be visible or hidden based on screen size or user need, and accommodate different app layouts.
Usage & Content Guidelines Content Guidelines
Use full-width dividers to separate groups of destinations. Don’t use dividers to separate individual destinations.
Don’t apply icons to some destinations and not others. Icons should be used for all destinations, or none.
