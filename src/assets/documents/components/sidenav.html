Sidenav
The sidenav components are designed to add collapsible side content that are anchored to the left or right edge of the screen.
Examples Examples
Sidenavs are anchored to the left or right edge of the screen. They are a fixed width and typically span the height of the screen. Their dimensions depend on how the app’s layout is subdivided into UI regions.

Best Practices Best Practices
Sidenav should:
  • Supporting: Sidenavs contain content that supplements the screen’s primary UI region
  • Flexible: Sidenavs display a wide variety of content and layouts.
  • Contextual: Sidenavs can be visible or hidden based on screen size or user need, and they often become bottom sheets on mobile.
Usage & Content guidelines Content guidelines
A visible scrim informs users they cannot interact with the rest of the screen. Don’t use an invisible scrim can mislead users about their ability to interact with the rest of the screen.
