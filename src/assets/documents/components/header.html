Header
Header appears on top all other surfaces in an app, displaying information and actions globally or relating to the current screen.
Examples Examples
Header are a special kind of toolbar that’s used for branding, navigation, search, and actions.
Application switcher in header is used for switching between applications within a product.

Best Practices Best Practices
Header should:
  • Persistent: Header appear at the top and bottom of each screen in an app, and can disappear upon scroll.
  • Guiding: Header provide a reliable way to guide users through an app.
  • Consistent: Header have a consistent position and content to increase familiarity.
  • Immediate: Header can be accessed from any scroll position.
Usage & Content Guidelines Content Guidelines
If title text is long, use a prominent app bar and wrap the title to two lines. Don’t truncate text, hiding the full title may cause misunderstanding. Don’t shrink text to fit on a single line.
