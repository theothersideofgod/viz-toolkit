Best Practices Best Practices
Footer should:
  • Persistent: Footer appear at the top and bottom of each screen in an app, and can disappear upon scroll.
  • Guiding: Footer provide a reliable way to guide users through an app.
  • Consistent: Footer have a consistent position and content to increase familiarity.
  • Immediate: Footer can be accessed from any scroll position.
