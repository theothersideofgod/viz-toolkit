Feedback Indicator
Feedback indicator is used to inform user about important changes or persistent conditions. Feedback indicator shows at the top of the page or section they apply to.
Examples Examples
Use to communicate problems that need to be resolved.
Use to display information that needs attention or needs to be taken action.
Use to inform when actions are successfully completed.

Best Practices Best Practices
Feedback indicator should:
  • Focus on a single theme, piece of information, or required action to avoid overwhelming users.
  • Be concise and scannable – users shouldn’t need to spend a lot of time figuring out what they need to know and do.
  • Be limited to a few important calls to action with no more than one primary action.
Usage & Content Guidelines Content Guidelines
Body content should be concise, keep content to 1 to 2 sentences where possible, clarify the benefit of the main task
