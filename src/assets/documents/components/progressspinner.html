Progress spinner
Circular progress indicators display progress by animating an indicator along an invisible circular track in a clockwise direction. They can be applied directly to a surface, such as a button or card.

Best Practices Best Practices
Progress spinnder should:
  • Determinate circular indicators fill the invisible, circular track with color, as the indicator moves from 0 to 360 degrees.
  • Indeterminate circular indicators grow and shrink in size while moving along the invisible track.
Usage & Content Guidelines Content Guidelines
Use circular indicators for short, indeterminate activities ( < 5 seconds) requiring user attention. Don’t apply progress indicators to every button in your app. They can unnecessarily interrupt the user’s task.
